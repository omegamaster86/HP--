About us

Company Description

We have a multinational and multilingual team of professionals that boast expert knowledge of Japanese pharmaceuticals. Over the years, our group of experts has developed a large network within the pharmaceutical industry, including strong relationships with many distributors and pharmacies throughout Japan.

Using our position as a GDP-compliant wholesaler and distributor of ethical drugs and prescription medicines, we aim to develop a business that can contribute to creating a society where people can receive better medical care.

Mission Statement

Japan Pharma Inc. strives to improve the health and development of society through responsible, best-in-class sourcing services, all while reducing costs for our partners and their customers. Success in this field will help us achieve our ultimate goal, which is to provide better medical care for each patient.

Management Policy

1. Creating a corporate culture trusted by society.

2. Continue to be an indispensable presence for our partner companies and society.

3. Cultivating a sincere and open-minded corporate culture and continuing to develop highly creative human resources.

Our Services

Looking towards the future, we pledge our efforts to creating a society where better medical care can be provided to patients in need. We are providing better value to our international partners by realizing improvements in accuracy and operational efficiency in our logistical services.

Sourcing and Procurement Services

With our vast experience in sourcing, as well as a reliable network of trusted suppliers within the industry, we are able to easily source and procure the medicines that ourpartners desire.

Logistics Services

We provide optimized logistical services to our global partners. Our team of specialists will analyze each partner's needs case by case in order to strategize the best logistics route.
