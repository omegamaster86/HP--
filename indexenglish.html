About us

company description

We are developing our business to create a society where everyone can receive better medical care through the "prescription drug wholesale business".

Management philosophy

We contribute to people's health and social development through distribution.

Management policy

1. Creation of a corporate culture trusted by society.

2. Continue to be the one and only existence for partner companies.

3. Cultivate a sincere and open-minded corporate culture and develop highly creative human resources.

Service contents

Aiming for a society where better medical care can be provided to consumers through innovation from logistics to the world of medical care. We are realizing the creation of distribution value by achieving both accuracy improvement and operational efficiency improvement in pharmaceutical distribution through logistics innovation.

Logistics that support stable supply

Realize optimization of logistics and cost reduction. We are doing business with many partner companies all over the country, and we have secured worldwide logistics.
